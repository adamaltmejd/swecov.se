title: SWECOV
---
SWECOV is a multidisciplinary research collaboration focused on using quantitative methods and comprehensive register data about the whole Swedish population to answer important questions about the consequences of the pandemic.

Read about all our ongoing and published research under [Research](/projects/). SWECOV is open to researchers who are interested in conributing to answering our broad research questions. Participation in SWECOV includes access to our extensive administrative data. For more information about how to apply to participate in the project, look under the [Join SWECOV](/participation/) page.
