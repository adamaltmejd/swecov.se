SWECOV hosts a yearly research workshop where we invite project members and other researchers working on the COVID-19 pandemic to present and discuss their work. The workshops provide an opportunity for scholars working in different fields to network and share their knowledge as well as discuss hands-on issues with experts in economics, demography, epidemiology and medicine, and develop new research ideas and collaborations. During the workshops, we also discuss future directions for the project.
